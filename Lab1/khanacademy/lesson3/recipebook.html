Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, milk, egg, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter into the pan.
  4. Cook until golden on both sides.

Source: AllRecipes – Pancakes

Spaghetti

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200 g
Tomato sauce 1 cup
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Mix spaghetti with sauce and serve.

Source: BBC Good Food – Spaghetti

Fruit Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Apple 1
Banana 1
Orange 1
Honey 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Wash and cut all fruits.
  2. Put fruits into a bowl.
  3. Add honey.
  4. Mix well and serve.

Source: BBC Good Food – Fruit Salad
